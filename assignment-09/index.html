Hello there, virtual friend. It appears that by some fate of destiny
you have been brought to the wonderful land of gaming, or perhaps are
just in need of a communication system for a wonderful group of friends.
Well have no fear! Discord can offer such services. Simply
follow the steps below to create a wonderful group chat/gathering for you
and your friends. Let us begin below! To start, please begin by either downloading
the Discord app or opening it up on your browser (either will work).

Materials

To begin, you will need the following:

  • A functioning computer
  • WiFi/stable internet connection
  • A web browser (such as Google)

    • Step 1

    Now that you are all settled at your computer and ready to work, it is time
    to begin! Start by either downloading discord or opening up discord up
    in a seperate web browser (which can be done by clicking the link above).

    Once this is open, you are going to click the "Plus" button below to create a new
    server. It should look something like this:



    Ahah! Progress! Now we will be greeted with a prompt offering us several templates to
    choose from. However, in our case we will be creating a custome one, so select "Create My Own!"



    It will also give you the option to either create one for you and your friends,
    or for a club or community. Choose whichever one you need for you discord purposes!
    Finally, you will be asked to create a name and add a photo. Keep in mind this can be
    changed later if you wish. And that conludes step one!
    Next we will look at our server, and all of the different things
    we can add to it!

    Step 2

    I'm glad to see you have made it this far! This is where it starts to get fun, so congrats!
    Assuming you are starting from where we left off from last section, you should be looking at
    something similar to what I have below:



    I have taken point of several things in this screen. As you will see there is a text channel
    and voice channel. These are pretty self explanitory, as the text channel you can type in,
    and the voice channel you can hop in and speak to people using a microphone connected to your PC or Mac.
    By clikcing the plus signs above either group of channels you can add a new one, as well as many as you'd like!
    Finally, you can also make categories, simiar to folders, for channels. This is done by right clicking anywhere
    in the channel screen, and selecting "Create Category"    .
