Popular 1980s Consoles



A lot was happening in the 1980's. People were starting to branch out
and accept more things, fashion was changing drastically, and technology
was growing faster than ever. However perhaps the most exciting thing was the
boom of video games. Arcades were busy, making millions, and eventually
the first home console came out. Below are some of the most popular home
consoles that revolutionized gaming.




Source: Evan-Amos, Wikipedia.

Above is the first NES system to come out internationally. It featured
games like Mario, Zelda, and more that made the game console incredibly
popular for all age groups, which was a big selling point for Nintendo.




Source: Evan-Amos, Wikipedia.

And here we have an Atari 7800. This model was the next gen of it's previous
versions (the 2600 and 5200), and is special because it was technically
the first console to have backwards combatibility for it's games.
