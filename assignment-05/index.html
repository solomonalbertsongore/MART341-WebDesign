The Lord of the Rings

J.R.R. Tolkein

A group of heroes known as The Fellowship of the Ring sets out in order
to destroy an all powerful ring in order to stop perhaps the most
dangerous being in Middle Earth, Sauron. This world has many races
such as elfs, dwarves, and hobbits, while also featuring normal humans.
As the brave group travels across Middle Earth they participate in large
battles, of both monsters and men, and much more.
