Ingredients

  • Slice of salmon
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • Brussel sprouts
  • Green pepper
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • Sprinkle of himalayn sea salt
  • Sprinkle of tumeric
  • Sprinkle of roasted garlic and herbs seasoning

Directions

  1. Start by cooking the rice in either a rice cooker or pot on the stove. This will probably take around 20 minutes.
  2. After the rice is started pull out the salmon and wash off the front and back with cool water. Place in a paper towel and damp off both sides. After that is done, combine all of the spices and sauces (everything after "green pepper") into a bowl
    (mix exteremely well) and place salmon inside. Let it sit or marinate for ideally 20 minutes (10 is fine too). Mix marinade up onto salmon every so often, and be sure to let it sit on both sides.
  3. After salmon has sat in the marinade for a while cook it on a heated cast iron pan with medium heat and extra virgin olive oil for 5 minutes each side.
  4. While the salmon is cooking cook the brussel sprouts and pepper in a seperate pan. Season with salt, and cook with extra virgin olive oil on medium heat.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
