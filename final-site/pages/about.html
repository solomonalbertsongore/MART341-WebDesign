About Me

Hello!

My name is Solomon (however many call me Solly). I'm from a small town in Montana, called Lewistown, and I'm currently a student at the University of Montana! For my degree, I chose to study Media Arts, with a focus in Game Design and Interactive Media. This website was developed as a project, and is now acting as my portfolio as well!

As you might be able to tell I have quite a bit of interests. I love to do pretty much anything, from gaming all the way to hiking. If I had to reccomend a game to you, it'd probably be Skyrim by Bethesda! I also have interests in music, photography, voice-acting, acting, 3D-modeling, game design (of course), cooking healthy food, fitness, and more!

If you go to my Portfolio on this site you will find all of my original works. I hope you enjoy this website and the day your reading it on!
